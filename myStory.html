Chhotu Kolhapuri is a household name amongst fast food lovers. We have taken our food culture to new heights. We are pioneers of Indian fast food culture as every product in our menu can be serve within 2 to 5 min at the counter with 8 variants of Vada Paav, parathas, kulfis, bhel, pattices, sabudana vada which is ideal during fasting, we have large variety of wraps and rolls, hot and cold beverages, deserts, ready to eat snacks, walky talky, dabeli and many more products with trending mayonnaise and cheese variants. We always give our best and keep trying to give most amazing experience to our patrons.

Chhotu Kolhapuri was establish in the year 2010. In last decade company has grown very fast. Now Chhotu Kolhapuri has its presence across India. We have our own fleet of refrigerated delivery vehicles, manufacturing facility equipped with state of the art Rapid Freezing Technology.

We have 3 state of the art manufacturing facilities, 2 at Satara near Kolhapur and 1 at Aurangabad with production capacity of 1200 ton, 2000 ton and 2400 ton respectively. This huge production capability allows us to compete with all local market competition, maintain pocket friendly rates of products and keep our raw material prices at minimum without compromising the quality of food.

Chhotu Kolhapuri strive to continue its journey to be best for customers, both internal and external. We are entitled to preserve and respect Kolhapuri Tradition through our products. We take this opportunity to thank our precious customer for unconditional love for our products, this motivates us to give best service and enable us to provide best quality products with value for their money.
