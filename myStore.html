Chhotu Kolhapuri store can be described as small wonder. It is compact and design that attract everyone. In mere 150 sq. ft. to 300 sq. ft. you can generate maximum profit from business with minimum investment.

We have ergonomic store design which allows to prepare and serve wide range of products in very less space and in time span of 2 to 5 min, enables workers to work with ease and comfortably.

We make sure that we have family friendly environment at our store with help of our store designing and site selection.

We keep in mind customers and workers safety while designing the store by following the safety norm set by fire safety department, FSSAI and other regulatory authorities of government.

Hygienic

Store Design

We in Chhotu Kolhapuri believe that “Serving safe food, it’s not an option it’s an obligation”. We do not leave any stone unturned when it comes to hygiene and food safety. We make sure we follow all norms set by FSSAI to serve our customer safe and healthy food.

While designing and choosing the machines, equipment, working table and utensils of store we keep hygiene at our top priority. All our machines, equipment, working table and utensils are made up of stainless steel which is easy to clean and safe to use in humid condition of kitchen.

After years of research and study we have observed that pests do not crawl up on stainless steel surface because of smoothness of surface and they see their reflection in stainless steel surface so they avoid climbing on to it and it will keep pests away from kitchen and service area.

We design and craft our working table and machine in such way that there is no room for pests to hide and reproduce. We fix wall tiles in store including service area which is easy to clean and to keep pests away.

ERGONOMIC

STORE DESIGN

Over the years we have developed expertise in ergonomic store set up. We have design our store set up in such a way that one can prepare and serve maximum products in minimum space and within very less time span. That means you choose any product from our vast menu and you get lightning fast service at our store.

While designing the store set up we have given great though on workers safety and comfort, the store set up is easy to clean, with all set up in store workers can move around easily in store. This design reduces the changes of mishaps and accidents within store, hence provide safe and comfortable environment to workers and our customers as well.
